Church-Service Missionary

The Church-service mission program includes some full-time opportunities that usually range from six months to two years of service, depending on the current needs and availability.

Tens of thousands of Church-service missionaries are needed every year to answer the call and devote the time they have to serving. Specific calls may vary, but all Church-service missions work to provide relief, comfort, and support to people around the world.
